The Secret Life Of Pets

The Plot

This is a movie that follows the story of two dogs that get into a big fight over their owner and end up getting captured by Animal Control, getting lost in the sewers, getting attacked by an evil bunny and his crew. They're called the Flushed Pets! It's an inspiring movie that tells us that family always sticks together. Nothing can separate the true bond of family and adventure.

This is Gidget she is one of the characters in the movie. She's in love with Max and helps rescue him from the sewers, she also loves watching telenovelas. She's adorable, and is voiced by Jenny Slate.

This is Max he is the main character he gets in a big fight with Duke and they end up in the sewers. He is so cute, and is voiced by Louis C.K.

This is Duke and he is Max's public enemy number one. His owner Katie brings him one day unexpectedly and they right away get into a big fight and decide that they don't like each other. They both get lost in the sewers and then become best friends after this big adventure. He is annoying at first but then he gets nice, he's voiced by Eric Stonestreet.

The Characters

  • Max (main character)
  • Duke (secondary character)
  • Kate (Max's and Duke's owner)
  • Gidget (Max's secret admirer)
  • Snowball (Boss of the Flushed Pets)
